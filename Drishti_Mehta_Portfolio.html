01

Building a VC brand on LinkedIn — from scratch

BeyondSeed Venture Solutions  ·  May 2025 – Present

Brand Strategy LinkedIn Growth Content
+

BeyondSeed needed to cut through a crowded VC landscape and position itself as a founder-first, value-driven partner — not just another cheque-writer. When I joined, the LinkedIn page had 4,000 followers and minimal engagement. There was no content playbook, no brand voice, and no strategy.

I built the entire content ecosystem from zero: defined brand pillars, created a hook-led posting framework, and developed a content calendar that balanced investor communication, founder storytelling, and ecosystem thought leadership. Every post was engineered to serve two audiences simultaneously — founders evaluating BeyondSeed as a partner, and LPs watching signal quality.

The results came fast. Within months, engagement jumped 42%, follower count crossed 10,000 (a 2.5× lift from 4,000), and multiple posts crossed 20K–50K impressions. The brand went from invisible to a recognised voice in India's startup ecosystem.

High-volume campaign execution for Indian Railways

F13 Technologies (Indian Railways)  ·  Feb 2024 – Feb 2025

Campaign Mgmt Scale Brand
+

Managing social media for Indian Railways meant operating at a scale most marketers rarely encounter — a 100K+ audience, 25+ campaigns per month, and zero margin for error. I was responsible for both Central and Western Railway's digital presence, which meant two distinct brand voices, two audiences, and twice the output.

The challenge wasn't creativity — it was systems. I built a campaign framework that allowed consistent output at volume: content templates, an approval workflow, and a monthly calendar structure that could flex for breaking news, festival campaigns, and special train announcements simultaneously. Festival-based storytelling became a major engagement driver, turning routine communication into cultural moments audiences actually looked forward to.

The result: 30% engagement rate improvement and a sustained 25+ campaign cadence every single month — without quality degradation.

03

Real-time content strategy at the National Games Goa 2023

Tattva Creations  ·  Sep – Nov 2023

Live Events Content Growth
+

Live event content is a different discipline entirely. You're creating, editing, and publishing while the event is happening — no second takes, no overnight turnarounds. At the National Games Goa 2023, I led on-ground content creation across all major sporting events at a national-scale competition.

The strategy was built around two things: speed and authenticity. We chose rawness over polish — real athlete moments, crowd energy, behind-the-scenes access. This approach built trust with audiences who were tired of over-produced sports content. I designed a real-time publishing workflow that let us post within minutes of key moments, keeping audiences engaged between events and coming back for more.

The campaign delivered a 45% engagement lift during the live period and 30% follower growth — numbers that held even after the event ended, showing the content had built genuine audience relationships, not just spike traffic.
